Recipe for preparing white rice

White rice is prepared with different types of stews.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice.
  • 2cups of water.
  • 1 tablespoon of oil.
  • A pinch of salt.

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice until the water becomes clearer.
  2. Put a pot on medium heat.
  3. Add the rice and stir it for 10 seconds.
  4. Pour 2 cups of rice and a pinch of salt.
  5. Pour the oil and cover the pot.
  6. Turn off the heat and let it sit covered for 10 minutes.
